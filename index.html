Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Knowledge Nexus

What is Knowledge Nexus?

Knowledge Nexus is an AI-powered knowledge management platform that transforms your documents into a searchable, intelligent knowledge base. Upload PDFs, documents, images, and more - then ask questions in natural language and get instant answers. Perfect for researchers, families, and enterprises.

Is there a free tier?

Yes! Our Explorer tier is completely free forever and includes 500 documents, 500MB storage, and 50 AI queries per month. No credit card required. Perfect for trying out the platform and personal use.

Can I self-host Knowledge Nexus?

Yes! Our Enterprise tier supports full self-hosted deployment. Your data never leaves your servers. We support air-gapped deployments, bring-your-own-LLM configurations (Ollama, vLLM), and custom security policies. Deploy with Docker in minutes.

What file types are supported?

Knowledge Nexus supports PDF, Word documents (.doc, .docx), text files, Markdown, images (with OCR), Excel spreadsheets, PowerPoint presentations, and more. Our desktop agent can also index files directly from your local computer.

How does the AI search work?

Knowledge Nexus uses advanced RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) technology. Your documents are converted into semantic embeddings, allowing the AI to understand meaning, not just keywords. Ask questions like "What did the contract say about termination?" and get accurate, contextual answers with citations.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption, SOC 2 compliant infrastructure, and never train AI models on your data. Enterprise customers can self-host for complete data sovereignty. We're GDPR and HIPAA compliant.
